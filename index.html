ALKAƘARIN UWA

By Abubakar Abdulwahab

Part 1 – Barin Gida

Asabar ta yi tsakar rana lokacin da Amina ta shiga cikin ɗakin mahaifiyarta tana ɗauke da jakar makaranta. Idanunta sun cika da hawaye, zuciyarta cike da ruɗani. Ta san wannan rana ba za ta manta ba, saboda ita ce ranar farko da za ta bar gida ta koma babban birni don ci gaba da karatu. Mahaifiyarta ta rungume ta cikin ƙauna, tana shirin yin fice daga zuciyarta ta bar mata addu'a da nasihu. “Ki kasance mai hakuri, ki bi gaskiya, kuma ki yi amfani da iliminki,” ta ce a hankali.

Part 2 – Nasara da Nisa

Bayan tafiya mai nisa, Amina ta isa birnin Lagos, inda ta fara sabon babin rayuwarta. Kamar yadda ta yi tsammani, birnin ya yi cike da hayaniya, amma akwai wani sabon yanayi na dama da take jin daɗin ganewa. Ta samu wurin zama a ɗaki mai kyau kusa da makaranta, kuma abokai da yawa sun fara zuwa gare ta. Duk da haka, zuciyarta tana kewar gida, kuma tana yawan tunanin mahaifiyarta da kaninta, Fatima.

Part 3 – Gida Mai Hawa Biyu

Bayan wata shida, Amina ta fara samun nasarori a makaranta. Malamai suna yabawa da jajircewarta, kuma wasu daga cikin abokanta suna neman shawararta akan karatu. Duk da haka, gida mai hawa biyu da mahaifiyarta ke ciki ya ci gaba da kasancewa cikin tunaninta. Duk lokacin da ta dawo gida don hutu, tana jin farin ciki mai yawa, tana kallon yadda komai ya canza amma akwai abubuwa da ba su canza ba.

Part 4 – Nadama

Wata rana Amina ta yi kuskure a cikin aikinta na makaranta, wanda ya sa ta ji nadama sosai. Ta san cewa wasu daga cikin abokanta sun rasa amincewa da ita saboda wannan kuskure. Duk da haka, ta yanke shawarar yin gyara, ta bayyana gaskiya ga malamai, kuma ta dauki alhakin aikinta. Wannan kwarewa ta koya mata cewa nadama da gaskiya sune hanyoyi mafi kyau na samun amincewa da girmamawa.

Part 5 – Asirin Sadaka

A cikin wannan sashe, Amina ta gano wani sirrin mahaifiyarta wanda ya shafi taimakon mabukata a unguwarsu. Mahaifiyarta ta yi amfani da dukiyar gida don taimaka wa marasa galihu ba tare da wani ya sani ba. Wannan ya koyar da Amina muhimmancin tausayi da sadaka, kuma ya canza tunaninta game da yadda mutum zai rayu cikin jinƙai da taimako.

Part 6 – Gidan Gaskiya

Bayan wasu shekaru, Amina ta dawo gida domin hutu. Ta lura cewa gida ya canza, amma gaskiya da kauna tsakanin iyalanta ba su canza ba. Ta fahimci cewa koda rayuwa ta kawo canje-canje, gidan gaskiya shine inda zuciya take samun nutsuwa. Ta yanke shawarar ci gaba da aiki tukuru, amma koyaushe za ta dawo gida domin samun kwanciyar hankali.

Part 7 – Sirrin Wasiƙa Ta Ƙarshe

Kwanaki kafin karshen makaranta, Amina ta samu wasiƙa daga mahaifiyarta, wacce ta bayyana cewa duk gwagwarmayar da ta sha tana da dalili mai zurfi. Wasiƙar ta ƙarfafa Amina, tana tuna mata cewa ƙoƙari, gaskiya, da tausayi sune ginshiƙan rayuwa. Wannan shi ne sirrin ƙarshe da zai taimaka mata ta zama cikakkiyar mace mai hikima da jinƙai.
